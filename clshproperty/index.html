About Us

CLSH Property Ltd is a full-service property company based in Greater Manchester, enchancing our clients quality of life through hands-off property investment and solutions, maximising returns on individual properties and property portfolios.

CLSH Property Ltd has been formed by company director Caroline Hume, who has over ten years of experience working for global companies helping them to maximise their turnover. After merging her skills with her partner in the IT industry, and creating a business that generates six figures profit within two years, she was then able to pursue her passion in the property industry.

Having success in renovating and selling property, she has now also turned her focus to the property rental market. Working closely with mentors and investors in London, she has adopted a system that maximises rental income while maintaining property to a high standard.
